What is ban.tu?

Join us in gathering small efforts for a greater purpose as we reinvent charity management in Singapore

ban.tu

was a name inspired by the Malay phrase “untuk membantu”, which translate as “to help”.
The ban.tu mission is simple: to bring together good work and good people who are passionately involved in the causes they champion.
Good people should stay connected & engaged with the groups they are volunteering their time with – and we provide VWOs the platform to do so.

We know you're busy in school...

but you want to contribute to the causes you care for
The ban.tu way is to remain up close and personal with the social causes you spend your Sunday afternoons at.

Everything is made better when you have a real relationship with the people & places you truly care about.

We're all different in the causes we care about...

but we have the same heart to help others. We can help you to stay connected to your causes and meet other awesome people just like you!
