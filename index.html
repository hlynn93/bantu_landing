What is ban.tu?

ban.tu is a charity management platform that aims to solve the problem of volunteer retention in Singapore.

ban.tu

Why ban.tu?
ban.tu is a name inspired by the Malay phrase for “to help”. We help to bring volunteers and welfare organisations together in providing sustained assistance to people in need.
Problem
High turnover rate of volunteers. This creates logistical problems for welfare organisations, and deepens the social disconnection between the people-in-need and the larger community.
Solution
ban.tu connects you to organisations that you find accessible, and makes it easy for you to remain actively involved with the communities and causes you care about.
